 In Atomic Habits, James Clear outlines a four-step model for human behavior that can be used to create new habits and achieve success. He explains how emotions and desires drive our thoughts and actions, and provides tactics such as implementation intentions, habit stacking, the Law of Least Effort, the Two-Minute Rule, and the Career Best Effort program to help readers go from being merely good to being truly great. He emphasizes the importance of changing our identity and making habits attractive, easy, and satisfying in order to form lasting habits.
